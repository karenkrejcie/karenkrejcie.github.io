The Story

My purpose in pursuing the certificate in Coding through MITxpro is to continue my life's adventure with Coding. I started this journey a few decades ago. It started as an adventure into Accounting, but I convinced myself I'd get bored of debiting and crediting every day for the rest of the life. Little did I know at that point that Accounting could have been more.

I pivoted. I pivot all the time throughout my life's adventure. I took the path down programming at the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. I never looked back. I loved the business sense married with the technology. And that's where my real professional adventure began. I parlayed my success at Indiana University to the corporate world working in IT at a Fortune 500 corporation for nearly a decade. Then I got tired of the corporate IT life and being on call and tending to all needs 24/7.

Pivot #2. I was teaching part-time at a local technical/community college throughout my years of corporate IT. I was consistently put in the place of training the new person at work. When I let these two worlds collide, I decided I wanted to commit to teaching at the technical/community college level. I've never had to pivot, career- wise again.

More than a decade later (closer to two actually), I'm still committed to providing a quality, technical 2-year education to students of all walks of life. As I tell them in the classroom, "the learning never ends." That's why I'm here.

Here's what I have to show for working full-time, mentoring a VEX High School robotics team (who was a 9x world qualifier!) and taking a class simultaneously.
